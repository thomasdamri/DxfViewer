Defect description

Valve handle exhibits progressive misalignment during actuation, resulting in increased torque requirements and inconsistent positioning at end states.

Observed deviation from nominal alignment exceeds acceptable tolerance thresholds under sustained cyclic load.

Defect nature

Mechanical wear and fatigue within the valve stem coupling assembly, likely caused by prolonged exposure to cyclic stress and insufficient lubrication.

Secondary contributing factors may include minor deformation of the coupling socket under thermal expansion.

Solution

Isolate and depressurise the affected system prior to intervention.

Disassemble the valve handle and inspect the stem coupling for signs of wear, deformation, or material degradation. Replace worn components per maintenance schedule.
